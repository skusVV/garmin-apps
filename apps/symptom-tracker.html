Symptom Tracker

Watch App Health Pain Log

Body Battery, sleep, and HR are great — but they don't tell your doctor when your knee ached, when the migraine hit, or what triggered yesterday's stomach pain. Symptom Tracker is a glanceable symptom and pain log built for your Garmin watch. No typing, no cloud sync — entries stay on your wrist. When you walk into the doctor's office, one tap emails your whole log to them.

One-tap logging

Walk a 4-step flow in under 15 seconds:

  • Where: head, face, neck, shoulder, chest, upper back, lower back, abdomen, arm, hand, leg, or foot
  • How it feels: aching, sharp, burning, throbbing, stiff, tingling, numb, cramping, itchy, or other
  • Severity: 0 to 10
  • Context: poor sleep, stress, alcohol, caffeine, exercise, dehydration, menstrual cycle, illness, medication, food

Views

  • Calendar — monthly heatmap showing which days had symptoms and how severe; tap any day for full detail
  • Edit & Delete — open any past entry and correct every field, or remove it entirely
  • Export — press once to email your full log: every timestamp, body part, severity, and context tag
  • Glance — days since last symptom plus most recent entry summary at a glance

Who it's for

  • Chronic pain sufferers tracking trigger patterns
  • Migraine and headache sufferers
  • People with arthritis, fibromyalgia, IBS, or any flare-prone condition
  • Athletes monitoring overuse, niggles, and recovery
  • Anyone preparing for a specialist appointment with concrete data instead of memory

Privacy

  • Data lives on your watch — no account, no login, no auto-sync, no analytics
  • Email export only fires when you tap Export, to the address you set

Compatible with modern Garmin AMOLED watches: Venu 3/3s/4, Vivoactive 5/6, Forerunner 265/570/965/970, Fenix 8, Fenix E, Epix Gen 2 & Pro, MARQ Gen 2, D2 Mach. Requires Connect IQ API 5.2+.

Not a medical device. For personal tracking only — does not diagnose, treat, or replace medical advice.
