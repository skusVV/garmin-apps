Symptom Tracker

Watch App Health Pain Log

Symptom Tracker is a symptom and pain log for compatible Garmin AMOLED watches. No typing, no cloud sync — log body part, sensation type, severity (0–10), and context tags in under 15 seconds. When you walk into the doctor's office, one tap emails your complete log to them.

One-tap logging flow

  • Where: head, face, neck, shoulder, chest, upper/lower back, abdomen, arm, hand, leg, or foot
  • How it feels: aching, sharp, burning, throbbing, stiff, tingling, numb, cramping, itchy, or other
  • Severity: 0 to 10
  • Context: poor sleep, stress, alcohol, caffeine, exercise, dehydration, menstrual cycle, illness, medication, or food

Views

  • Calendar — monthly heatmap showing which days had symptoms and severity; tap any day for full detail
  • Edit & Delete — correct any past entry or remove it entirely
  • Export — one-tap email of your full log: every timestamp, body part, severity, and context tag
  • Glance — days since last symptom and most recent entry at a glance

Who it's for

  • People with chronic pain tracking trigger patterns
  • Arthritis, fibromyalgia, IBS, and flare-prone conditions
  • Athletes monitoring overuse injuries and recovery
  • Anyone preparing for a specialist appointment with concrete data
  • Anyone whose doctor has asked them to "keep a symptom diary"

Symptom Tracker is for personal tracking and informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or replace medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for any health concerns.

How to install Symptom Tracker on your Garmin watch

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for Symptom Tracker
  2. Tap Try to start the 7-day free trial
  3. Sync your Garmin watch via the Garmin Connect app
  4. To enable email export: Garmin Connect → My Apps → Symptom Tracker → Settings → enter your email address
  5. Open the app on your watch and tap to log your first symptom

Frequently asked questions

Can I send my symptom history to my doctor?

Yes. Open the Export view and tap once — your complete log (every entry with timestamp, body part, sensation, severity, and context tags) is emailed to the address you configured. You can forward it to your doctor, paste it into a spreadsheet, or file it for your records.

Is my symptom data private?

Yes. All data is stored on your watch only. There is no cloud account, no automatic sync, and no analytics collection. The email export only fires when you explicitly tap it in the Export view.

Can I correct a mistake in a logged entry?

Yes. Open any past entry from the Calendar view and edit every field — body part, sensation, severity, or context tags. You can also delete entries entirely. Your history stays accurate and trustworthy.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed when you send an email export.
