AI Coach

Watch App Health & Fitness AI-powered

Meet AI Coach — the first AI-powered fitness coach built directly into your Garmin watch.

Every morning, midday, and evening, AI Coach reads your body battery, stress, recovery time, resting heart rate, and completed workouts — then delivers one clear, actionable recommendation for your day. Pushed a hard 10km run yesterday? It'll tell you to back off. Body battery recovered overnight? It'll push you to go for it.

What makes it different

  • Advice updates automatically 3× per day — no tapping required
  • Detects your completed activities and refreshes instantly when you open the app
  • Understands your sport, goal, fitness level, and personality — you configure it once
  • Works entirely on your watch — no phone needed for daily updates

Personalize your coach

  • Primary sport: running, cycling, swimming, and more
  • Goal: health, fitness, race prep, weight loss
  • Experience level and coaching personality
  • Personalities: supportive, direct, data-driven, military, zen, or humorous
