AI Coach

Watch App Health & Fitness AI-powered

AI Coach turns your Garmin's sensor data into plain-language training advice. Three times a day — morning, midday, and evening — it reads your Body Battery, stress score, resting heart rate, VO2max, and weekly training load, then delivers a concise coaching insight tailored to your goal and sport.

Unlike generic wellness apps, AI Coach adapts to what your body actually did yesterday. Had a rough night? It backs off. Hit a recovery milestone? It pushes you forward. You set the goal, sport, experience level, and even the coaching personality — supportive, direct, or motivational.

Features

  • Morning insight, midday update, and evening recap every day
  • Reads Body Battery, stress, VO2max, resting HR, training status
  • Set your goal: endurance, weight loss, general fitness, race prep
  • Choose sport: running, cycling, triathlon, swimming, and more
  • Configurable coach personality and communication style
  • Works on wrist — no phone needed once synced
  • Optional context field for injuries, schedule constraints, or goals
