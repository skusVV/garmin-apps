AI Coach

Watch App Health & Fitness AI-powered

AI Coach is an AI-powered fitness coaching app for compatible Garmin watches. Every morning, midday, and evening it reads your body battery, stress score, recovery time, resting heart rate, and completed workouts — then delivers one clear, actionable coaching recommendation directly on your wrist.

Pushed a hard 10km run yesterday? It'll tell you to back off. Body battery recovered overnight? It'll push you to go for it. Configure your sport, goal, experience level, and coaching personality once — and AI Coach handles the daily guidance from there.

What makes it different

  • Advice updates automatically 3× per day — no tapping required
  • Detects your completed activities and refreshes instantly when you open the app
  • Understands your sport, goal, fitness level, and personality — configured once
  • Works entirely on your watch — no phone needed for daily updates

Personalise your coach

  • Primary sport: running, cycling, swimming, triathlon, and more
  • Goal: health, general fitness, race prep, or weight loss
  • Experience level: beginner to advanced
  • Coaching personality: supportive, direct, data-driven, military, zen, or humorous

AI Coach delivers general fitness guidance based on available Garmin health metrics. It is for wellness and informational purposes only and is not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional before making significant changes to your training or health routine.

Compatible Garmin watches

  • Forerunner series (255, 265, 955, 965, 970 and others)
  • Fēnix series (6, 7, 8 and variants)
  • Epix series (Gen 2, Pro)
  • Venu series (2, 3, 4)
  • Vívoactive series (4, 5, 6)
  • Instinct series (2, 2X Solar, 3, AMOLED)
  • Enduro series, MARQ Gen 2
  • Requires body battery and stress data — check your watch's sensor support

How to install AI Coach on your Garmin watch

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for AI Coach
  2. Tap Try to start the 48-hour free trial
  3. Sync your Garmin watch via the Garmin Connect app
  4. Open AI Coach on your watch
  5. Configure sport, goal, experience level, and coaching personality in Garmin Connect → My Apps → AI Coach → Settings

Frequently asked questions

How does AI Coach work on Garmin?

AI Coach syncs your Garmin health metrics — body battery, stress score, recovery time, resting heart rate, and workout history — and uses AI to generate one actionable coaching recommendation three times per day: morning, midday, and evening.

Does AI Coach need the phone for every update?

Coaching recommendations are generated after each data sync between your watch and the Garmin Connect app. Once synced, recommendations are available on your watch without an active phone connection until the next sync.

What metrics does AI Coach read?

Body battery, stress score, recovery time, resting heart rate, and recently completed activities. The more of these your watch tracks, the more tailored the coaching.

Is there a subscription?

No. AI Coach is a one-time $4.99 purchase after the 48-hour free trial. No monthly fees, no subscription, no recurring charges.
