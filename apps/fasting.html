Fasting Tracker

Widget Health Nutrition

The intermittent fasting tracker built for your wrist. Watch your biological phase shift in real time — from Fed State to Fat Burning, Ketosis, and Autophagy — as the progress ring fills toward your 16:8, 18:6, or 20:4 goal. No phone required, no account, fully offline.

Features

  • Live phase tracker: Fed State → Fat Burning → Ketosis → Autophagy → Peak Autophagy
  • Animated progress arc — fills second by second as your fast advances
  • Countdown to the next metabolic milestone
  • 7-day fasting history chart — see your consistency at a glance
  • Lifetime stats: total fasts, longest fast, average, streak
  • Glance view — check your fasting status without opening the app
  • Flexible plans: 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD, or custom target
  • Forgot to stop? Set the exact time you finished — adjust up to 16 hours back
  • Vibration the moment your target fast is complete
  • Runs offline — your data never leaves your watch

Fasting phases

  • Fed State (0h)
  • Digesting (4h)
  • Fat Burning (8h)
  • Crossover (12h)
  • Ketosis (14h)
  • Autophagy (20h)
  • Peak Autophagy (24h+)

Perfect for

  • Intermittent fasting beginners on 16:8
  • Experienced OMAD practitioners
  • Keto + IF combos
  • Longevity-focused fasters chasing autophagy benefits
