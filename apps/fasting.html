Intermittent Fasting

Widget Health Nutrition

The Intermittent Fasting widget brings fasting tracking to your wrist. Start or stop a fast with a button press, see exactly how much time remains in your fasting or eating window, and stay accountable with your running streak — all without reaching for your phone.

Configure your target fasting duration and fast start time in Garmin Connect settings. The widget handles the rest: live countdown, streak tracking, stats (total fasts, average duration, personal best), and reminders when your eating window is closing.

Features

  • Start and stop fasts directly from your watch
  • Live countdown for current fasting or eating window
  • Daily streak counter and total fast history
  • Adjust fast start time retroactively (back 15 min, 30 min, 1h, 2h)
  • Reminders when eating window is closing and when goal is reached
  • Configurable fasting duration and schedule
  • Stats view: total fasts, average duration, personal best
