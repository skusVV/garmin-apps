20-20-20 Eye Saver: Smart Vision Breaks

Widget Eye Health Desk Workers

Protect your vision and reduce digital eye strain with the 20-20-20 rule, right from your Garmin watch.

If you spend hours staring at a screen, you likely know the symptoms: dry eyes, blurred vision, headaches. The 20-20-20 rule — recommended by eye-care professionals — is simple: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet (6 meters) away for 20 seconds. This app automates it intelligently so you can stay focused on your work while we watch out for your eyes.

Core features

  • Smart 20-20-20 Timer — a gentle vibration every 20 minutes plus a clear 20-second rest countdown on your watch
  • Stress-Aware Postponement — reads your live stress score; if you're in deep focus or a high-stress moment, the alert is postponed so it doesn't break your flow (a Garmin-exclusive feature)
  • Movement Detection — walking around means you're not at your desk; the app resets the timer automatically so you're never buzzed unnecessarily
  • Do Not Disturb Aware — respects your watch's DND setting
  • Activity Aware — stays silent while you're recording a workout
  • Customisable Work Hours — set your daily schedule (e.g. 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM) and active days (every day or weekdays only)
  • Fully Adjustable — change the interval, rest duration, and step threshold from the Garmin Connect app on your phone

How to use

  • Install the app and open it once to activate it
  • The app runs quietly in the background during your working hours
  • When the reminder fires, look away from your screen for the countdown
  • Adjust your preferences — hours, days, intervals, step threshold — in the Garmin Connect app
