20-20-20 Eye Saver: Smart Vision Breaks

Widget Eye Health Desk Workers

20-20-20 Eye Saver is an eye strain prevention app for compatible Garmin watches. It automates the 20-20-20 rule recommended by optometrists: every 20 minutes of screen time, look at something at least 20 feet (6 meters) away for 20 seconds. The app handles this intelligently — pausing when you're stressed, moving, or in a workout — so you can stay focused while it watches out for your eyes.

Core features

  • Smart 20-20-20 timer — gentle wrist vibration every 20 minutes plus a 20-second rest countdown
  • Stress-Aware Postponement — reads your Garmin's live stress score; postpones alerts when you're in deep focus or a high-stress moment (Garmin-exclusive feature)
  • Movement Detection — walking means you're not at your desk; resets the timer automatically so you're never buzzed unnecessarily
  • Do Not Disturb aware — respects your watch's DND setting
  • Activity aware — stays silent during any recorded workout
  • Customisable work hours — set your daily schedule and active days (every day or weekdays only)
  • Fully adjustable — change interval, rest duration, and step threshold in Garmin Connect app settings

Frequently asked questions

What is the 20-20-20 rule?

The 20-20-20 rule is a guideline recommended by optometrists to reduce digital eye strain: every 20 minutes of screen time, look at something at least 20 feet (6 meters) away for 20 seconds. This relaxes the ciliary muscles that are held in a fixed position while focusing on a screen.

What is stress-aware postponement?

20-20-20 Eye Saver reads your Garmin's live stress score. If you're in a high-stress moment or deep focus session — above a configurable threshold — the reminder is automatically postponed. It fires when your stress level drops to a more suitable moment.

Does it work on weekends?

You can configure the app to alert on weekdays only, or every day. Set your active days in the Garmin Connect app settings.

Does it interrupt workouts?

No. The app automatically stays silent while you're recording any Garmin activity and resumes normal operation after the activity ends.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. A full 24-hour free trial is included. After the trial, unlock forever for $1.99 — one-time payment, no subscription.
