20-20-20

Widget Eye Health Desk Workers

The 20-20-20 rule is a well-established optometric guideline for reducing digital eye strain: every 20 minutes, look at something at least 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This app automates that habit entirely — you just wear your watch.

What makes it smarter than a simple timer: it reads your Garmin's stress score and step count, and automatically postpones alerts if you're already stressed or physically active. No interruptions during a workout or a high-pressure meeting.

Features

  • Automatic wrist reminders on a configurable interval (default 20 min)
  • Configurable rest duration (20–60 seconds)
  • Active only during your work hours — set start and end time
  • Choose active days: every day or weekdays only
  • Auto-pauses when Garmin stress score exceeds your threshold
  • Auto-pauses during physical activity based on step count
  • Enable/disable toggle without resetting other settings
