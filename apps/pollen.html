Pollen

Widget Allergy Outdoor

Pollen puts allergy season data on your wrist. The widget shows today's pollen levels for tree, grass, and weed, plus a 5-day forecast — so you can plan runs, rides, and outdoor sessions around the pollen calendar instead of reacting to symptoms after the fact.

Set your coordinates once in Garmin Connect settings. The app syncs automatically and shows last-updated time so you always know how fresh the data is. The glance view gives a one-line summary on supported watches.

Features

  • Today's pollen index: tree, grass, and weed separately
  • 5-day pollen forecast
  • Glance view for quick at-a-glance pollen level
  • Location-based via coordinates in Garmin Connect settings
  • Shows time of last data sync
  • Covers all major allergy seasons year-round
