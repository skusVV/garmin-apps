Pollen Pulse — 5-Day Allergy Forecast

Widget Allergy Outdoor

Pollen Pulse puts a real, daily pollen forecast on your watch — built for runners, cyclists, gardeners, and anyone who lives with seasonal allergies or hayfever.

What you get

  • At-a-glance widget: tree, grass, and weed levels for today, right on your home screen
  • Today in detail: the dominant pollen type plus the top 4 plants driving it (Birch, Pine, Oak, Ragweed, and more)
  • 5-day forecast: plan your runs and outdoor sessions around clean-air days
  • Daily auto-sync: opens the app and refreshes once per day — no fiddling
  • Manual refresh anytime from the update screen

Why you'll love it

  • Designed for glanceability — high-contrast colors, no clutter, no animations
  • Smart layout that adapts from 176px Instinct to 454px AMOLED
  • Tiny memory footprint, fast to open, easy on the battery
  • Works worldwide — set your coordinates once and you're done

Setup (1 minute)

  • Install the widget
  • In Garmin Connect IQ → My Apps → Pollen Pulse → Settings, enter your latitude and longitude
  • Open the widget — that's it

Compatible with 80+ Garmin watches: Forerunner, Fenix, Epix, Venu, Vivoactive, Marq, Enduro, Instinct, and more. Requires phone connection (Bluetooth) for daily data sync.
