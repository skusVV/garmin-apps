Pollen Pulse — 5-Day Allergy Forecast

Widget Allergy Outdoor

Pollen Pulse is a pollen forecast widget for compatible Garmin watches, built for runners, cyclists, gardeners, and anyone who manages seasonal allergies or hayfever. See today's tree, grass, and weed pollen levels — plus a 5-day forecast — directly on your wrist.

What you get

  • At-a-glance widget: tree, grass, and weed levels for today on your home screen
  • Today in detail: dominant pollen type plus the top 4 plants driving it (Birch, Pine, Oak, Ragweed, and more)
  • 5-day forecast: plan runs and outdoor sessions around clean-air days
  • Daily auto-sync: refreshes once per day when you open the app — no fiddling
  • Manual refresh available from the update screen at any time
  • High-contrast layout adapts from 176px Instinct to 454px AMOLED screens
  • Works worldwide — set your coordinates once

Pollen data is sourced from third-party providers and is for general awareness only. Individual allergy sensitivities vary significantly. Consult a healthcare professional for allergy diagnosis and personalised management advice.

Compatible Garmin watches

  • Fēnix series (5, 6, 7, 8 and variants)

Setup — 1 minute

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for Pollen Pulse
  2. Tap Try to start the 24-hour free trial
  3. In Garmin Connect → My Apps → Pollen Pulse → Settings, enter your latitude and longitude
  4. Sync your watch and open the widget — pollen data loads automatically

Frequently asked questions

Which pollen types does Pollen Pulse track?

Pollen Pulse tracks three main pollen categories: tree pollen (including Birch, Pine, Oak), grass pollen, and weed pollen (including Ragweed). The detail view shows the dominant type and the top 4 specific plants for your location.

How do I set my location for pollen data?

Enter your latitude and longitude once in Garmin Connect → My Apps → Pollen Pulse → Settings. The app uses these coordinates for all pollen data requests. You do not need to change this unless you move.

How often does pollen data update?

Pollen Pulse automatically syncs once per day when the widget is opened. You can manually refresh from the update screen at any time.

Does it work outside the US?

Yes. Pollen Pulse works worldwide wherever the underlying pollen data source has coverage. Simply set your local coordinates in settings.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Pollen Pulse includes a full 24-hour free trial with all features unlocked. After the trial, unlock forever for $3.99 — one-time payment, no subscription.
