UV Index

Watch App Health Sun Safety

UV Index doesn't just show a number — it personalizes the risk for your skin. Select your Fitzpatrick skin type (I through VI) and your location, and the app translates the raw UV index into actionable guidance: safe all day, protect after a certain hour, or high all day.

Useful year-round, especially for runners, cyclists, and outdoor athletes who train during peak UV hours without realizing cumulative exposure is adding up.

Features

  • Real-time UV index for your location
  • Personalized risk based on skin type (Fitzpatrick I–VI)
  • Clear status: safe all day / protect after X / high all day
  • Daily UV peak hour indicator
  • Location set via latitude/longitude in Garmin Connect
  • Lightweight and fast — always up to date after sync
