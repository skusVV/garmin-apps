UV Guard: Sun & Burn Forecast

Watch App Health Sun Safety

Know when to put on sunscreen — before you regret it.

UV Guard shows your real-time UV index, a personalized burn countdown for your skin type, and the exact hours to avoid direct sun — all from your Garmin watch. No phone needed. Most UV apps live on your phone. This one lives on your wrist, where it matters — mid-run, on the slopes, or out on the water.

What you get

  • Current UV index with color-coded risk level (Low → Extreme)
  • Burn time personalised to your skin type (Fitzpatrick scale I–VI) — fair skin and dark skin don't burn the same way
  • SPF recommendation for current conditions
  • 24-hour UV forecast chart so you can plan your day
  • Daily avoid window — exactly when UV peaks and you should seek shade
  • Glance view for an instant UV check without opening the app

Works on 81+ Garmin devices including Fenix 6/7/8, Epix, Forerunner 255/265/955/965/970, Instinct 3, MARQ 2, and Venu 2/3 series.

UV Guard is provided for informational and educational purposes only and is not intended as medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. UV forecasts and burn estimates are approximate and may vary based on individual conditions and environmental factors. Consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding safe sun exposure practices and skin health.
