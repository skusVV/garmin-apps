Headache Log

Watch App Health Migraine

Tired of trying to remember when your last headache hit, how bad it was, or what might have caused it? Headache Log is a one-tap headache and migraine logger for your Garmin watch — no phone needed, no typing, no fighting with an app while your head is pounding.

Tap once to log a headache. Pick severity (0–10). Choose "log & close" for a brief episode, or "start episode" to track duration. Add optional tags: aura, nausea, light or sound sensitivity, one-sided, throbbing, neck pain, or common triggers like skipped meals, poor sleep, alcohol, stress, caffeine, menstrual cycle, dehydration, strong smells, and exertion.

Every log automatically captures the Garmin data already on your wrist — body battery, stress level, resting heart rate, respiration, steps, and (on supported watches) barometric pressure and its recent trend. Over time the app surfaces patterns like "headaches when barometric pressure drops more than 3 hPa in 6 hours" or "headaches after poor sleep."

Views

  • Status — days since your last headache, count this month, log in one press
  • Calendar — month heatmap; swipe to any day and tap for full detail
  • Patterns — ranked correlations from your own data (unlocks at 10+ events)
  • Stats — avg severity, avg duration, total this month, most common tag
  • Export — send all your records by email when you want a copy off the watch

Privacy

  • All data stays on your watch — no cloud account, no third-party sharing
  • Optional email export sends a copy only when you tap it

Compatible with modern AMOLED Garmin watches — Venu 3/3s/4, Vivoactive 5/6, Forerunner 265/570/965/970, Fenix 8, Epix Gen 2 & Pro, MARQ Gen 2, and more. Requires Connect IQ API 5.2+.
