Headache Log

Watch App Health Migraine

Headache Log is a one-tap headache and migraine tracker for compatible Garmin AMOLED watches — no phone needed, no typing, no fighting with an app while your head is pounding. Tap once to log a headache, pick severity, and add optional context. Your watch captures the Garmin health data automatically.

Every log automatically captures the data already on your wrist: body battery, stress level, resting heart rate, respiration, steps, and (on supported watches) barometric pressure and its recent trend. Over time the app surfaces correlations like "headaches when barometric pressure drops more than 3 hPa in 6 hours."

Views

  • Status — days since your last headache, count this month, log in one press
  • Calendar — monthly heatmap; tap any day for a full entry view with time, severity, and tags
  • Patterns — ranked correlations from your own data (unlocks after 10 events)
  • Stats — average severity, average duration, total this month, most common trigger tag
  • Export — one-tap email of your complete log to share with your doctor

Privacy

  • All headache records are stored on your watch only
  • No cloud account, no server, no analytics
  • Email export only fires when you explicitly tap it

Headache Log is for personal tracking and informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding headache or migraine management.

How to install Headache Log on your Garmin watch

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for Headache Log
  2. Tap Try to start the 7-day free trial
  3. Sync your Garmin watch via the Garmin Connect app
  4. Open Headache Log on your watch and tap to log your first entry
  5. To enable email export: Garmin Connect → My Apps → Headache Log → Settings → enter your email address

Frequently asked questions

Can I share my headache log with my doctor?

Yes. The Export view sends your complete headache log — every timestamp, severity rating, tags, and automatically captured Garmin metrics — to your configured email address in one tap. You can forward this directly to your doctor or specialist.

What Garmin data does Headache Log capture automatically?

Each log entry automatically captures: body battery, stress level, resting heart rate, respiration rate, step count, and (on supported watches) barometric pressure and its recent trend. You don't need to enter any of these manually.

Is my headache data private?

Yes. All records are stored on your watch only. The app has no cloud sync, no server, and no analytics. The optional email export only sends data when you explicitly tap Export.

When does pattern detection unlock?

The Patterns view, which shows ranked correlations between your headaches and captured metrics, unlocks after you've logged 10 or more headache events.
