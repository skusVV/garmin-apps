Tai Chi Tracker

Watch App Mindfulness Martial Arts

Tai Chi deserves its own activity — not a Yoga entry in your feed. Tai Chi Tracker records every session as a dedicated Tai Chi activity in Garmin Connect, with a clean, native-style interface designed to match the look of Garmin's built-in workouts.

During practice you see a live duration timer and your real-time heart rate, confirming you're staying in a calm, mindful zone. When you finish, a post-session summary shows duration, average heart rate, average respiration rate, and average stress level — plus full-session charts for all three so you can see exactly how your body responded.

Frequently asked questions

Why does it save as Tai Chi instead of Yoga or Other?

Garmin Connect supports a dedicated Tai Chi activity type. Tai Chi Tracker uses it so your sessions appear correctly labelled in your activity history, training load, and weekly summaries — not lumped in with yoga or generic workouts.

What metrics are tracked?

During the session: live timer and real-time heart rate. Post-session summary shows duration, average heart rate, average respiration rate, and average stress level. You also get full-session charts for heart rate, respiration, and stress so you can see how your body responded across the entire practice.

Can I pause mid-session?

Yes. You can pause, resume, or discard a session at any time from the watch — using either buttons or the touchscreen, depending on your device.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Tai Chi Tracker includes a 72-hour free trial. After the trial, unlock forever for $1.99 — one-time payment, no subscription.
