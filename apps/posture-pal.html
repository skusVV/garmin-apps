Posture Pal

Watch App Health Ergonomics

Posture Pal is a set-it-and-forget-it app for people who sit at a desk. Once configured, it vibrates your wrist on a regular interval during your work hours — a gentle nudge to sit up straight, relax your shoulders, or adjust your screen position.

Configure active hours, interval frequency, and the reminder text shown on screen. Outside your active window, the app stays silent.

Features

  • Configurable start and end hour for active monitoring
  • Alert interval from 5 to 120 minutes
  • Custom reminder text shown on wrist
  • Enable/disable toggle without changing other settings
  • Wrist vibration alert — no sound, no screen flash
  • Runs in the background — no battery drain from active display
