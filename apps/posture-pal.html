Posture Pal: Smart Reminders

Watch App Health Ergonomics

Posture Pal taps your wrist throughout the day so you remember to sit up, roll your shoulders back, and reset your spine before stiffness sets in. The reminder fires at the interval you pick — five minutes for an intense focus session, an hour for a more relaxed nudge.

Built for office workers, developers, students, and anyone who spends long hours in front of a screen.

Features

  • Reminder interval from 5 to 120 minutes
  • Active hours window — alerts only fire during your work day
  • One-tap pause and resume from the watch
  • Auto-pauses while you're recording an activity
  • Respects Do Not Disturb
  • Custom alert text
