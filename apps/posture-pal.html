Posture Pal: Smart Reminders

Watch App Health Ergonomics

Posture Pal is a posture reminder app for compatible Garmin watches. It taps your wrist throughout the day so you remember to sit up, roll your shoulders back, and reset your spine before stiffness sets in. Built for office workers, developers, students, and anyone who spends long hours in front of a screen.

The reminder fires at the interval you pick — five minutes for an intense focus session, an hour for a more relaxed nudge. Outside your active window, the app stays completely silent.

Features

  • Reminder interval from 5 to 120 minutes — fully configurable
  • Active hours window — alerts only fire during your work day
  • One-tap pause and resume from the watch, no settings needed
  • Auto-pauses while you're recording any activity on your Garmin watch
  • Respects your watch's Do Not Disturb setting
  • Custom alert text shown on your wrist

Compatible Garmin watches

  • Compatible with a wide range of Garmin Connect IQ watches
  • Forerunner series (255, 265, 955, 965, 970 and others)
  • Fēnix series (6, 7, 8 and variants)
  • Epix series (Gen 2, Pro)
  • Venu series (2, 3, 4)
  • Vívoactive series (4, 5, 6)
  • Instinct series, Enduro, MARQ Gen 2
  • Requires Garmin Connect app to configure settings

How to install Posture Pal on your Garmin watch

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for Posture Pal
  2. Tap Try to start the 24-hour free trial
  3. Sync your Garmin watch via the Garmin Connect app
  4. Open Posture Pal on your watch once to activate it
  5. Set your interval and active hours in Garmin Connect → My Apps → Posture Pal → Settings

Frequently asked questions

Does Posture Pal drain the watch battery?

Posture Pal runs as a background app using Garmin's standard background framework. It does not keep the display active and has minimal impact on battery life.

Can I pause reminders without changing settings?

Yes. You can pause and resume reminders with a single tap from the watch at any time. Your interval and hours settings are preserved when you resume.

Does it interrupt workouts?

No. Posture Pal automatically pauses alerts while you're recording any activity on your Garmin watch and resumes when the activity ends.

Can I set it to only remind me on weekdays?

Yes. Configure your active days in Garmin Connect app settings — you can set specific start and end times for the active reminder window each day.
