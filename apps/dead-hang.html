Dead Hang Pro | Hands-Free Grip Timer

Watch App Strength Climbing

Dead Hang Pro turns your Garmin watch into a hands-free dead hang training companion for grip strength, hangboard climbing, and calisthenics.

Press START, grab the bar, hang. The watch counts down, buzzes when you go, blips every 30 seconds so you can track time without looking, and automatically stops when you drop. No fumbling for buttons mid-set.

What you get

  • Hands-free timer — countdown, blip every 30 seconds, auto-stop on drop detection
  • Haptic-driven workflow — train eyes-closed, no need to look at the watch
  • Saves as Strength Training activity in Garmin Connect — full FIT recording
  • Heart rate captured during the hang
  • Drop detection (~2.5G impact threshold)
  • Manual stop fallback — press BACK any time
  • Save / discard confirmation at the end of every set

How it works

  • Open Dead Hang Pro
  • Press START — 5-second countdown
  • Grab the bar — long buzz means GO
  • Hang — short blip every 30 seconds
  • Drop or press BACK — long buzz means done
  • SAVE to keep the activity, BACK to discard

Perfect for

  • Climbers and boulderers — hangboard sessions
  • Calisthenics athletes — dead hangs, grip endurance
  • CrossFit and pull-up training
  • Anyone working on grip strength, finger strength, or shoulder health
