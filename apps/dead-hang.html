Dead Hang Pro | Hands-Free Grip Timer

Watch App Strength Climbing

Dead Hang Pro is a hands-free grip strength timer for compatible Garmin watches. Press START, grab the bar, hang. The watch counts down, buzzes when you go, blips every 30 seconds so you can track time without looking, and automatically stops when you drop. No fumbling for buttons mid-set.

What you get

  • Hands-free timer — countdown, 30-second blips, auto-stop on drop detection
  • Haptic-driven workflow — train eyes-closed, no need to look at or touch the watch during a set
  • Saves as Strength Training activity in Garmin Connect — full FIT file recording
  • Heart rate captured during the hang
  • Drop detection at approximately 2.5G impact threshold
  • Manual stop fallback — press BACK any time
  • Save / discard confirmation at the end of every set

How it works

  1. Open Dead Hang Pro on your Garmin watch
  2. Press START — 5-second countdown begins
  3. Grab the bar — long buzz means GO
  4. Hang — short blip every 30 seconds counts your time
  5. Drop or press BACK — long buzz means the set is done
  6. SAVE to keep the activity in Garmin Connect, BACK to discard

Perfect for

  • Rock climbers and boulderers — hangboard training sessions
  • Calisthenics athletes — dead hangs and grip endurance work
  • CrossFit and pull-up training
  • Anyone building grip strength, finger strength, or shoulder health

Frequently asked questions

How does drop detection work?

Dead Hang Pro uses your watch's accelerometer. When the impact of landing after releasing the bar is detected — approximately 2.5G threshold — the timer automatically stops. You can also stop manually at any time by pressing BACK.

Does it save to Garmin Connect?

Yes. Each set is saved as a Strength Training activity in Garmin Connect with a full FIT file recording, including heart rate data captured throughout the hang.

Why not just use a stopwatch?

A standard stopwatch requires you to look at or tap the watch mid-hang, which breaks focus and can break your grip. Dead Hang Pro is entirely haptic — a buzz to start, 30-second blips to track time, and a buzz when you drop — so you never need to touch or look at the watch during a set.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Dead Hang Pro includes a 24-hour free trial. After the trial, unlock forever for $1.99 — one-time payment, no subscription.
