Cold Plunge Pro: Ice Bath & Shiver Protocol

Watch App Cold Exposure Recovery

Master cold exposure and unlock the full neurobiological benefits of ice baths and cold showers with Cold Plunge Pro.

Designed for deliberate cold exposure enthusiasts, the app guides you through the immersion phase and the crucial natural re-warming (shiver) phase — then tracks your progress over time so you can see your tolerance building.

Features

  • Guided Protocol — seamlessly transitions from preparation through cold immersion into a dedicated shiver phase to maximise metabolic benefits
  • Live Heart Rate — tracks HR min / avg / max on your wrist throughout the plunge
  • Streak Tracking — daily streak with a 1-day grace period so one missed day won't erase your momentum
  • Weekly Goal — set a weekly cold-time target (in minutes); the This Week screen turns green when you hit it
  • All-Time Stats — total sessions, total lifetime cold time, average session, and personal best
  • Milestone Celebrations — recognised at 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, and 500 sessions, and 1, 5, 10, 25 hours of total cold exposure
  • Garmin Connect Integration — every session saved as an activity with custom immersion and shiver fields
  • Smart Alerts — warns you if you're plunging late in the evening when cold exposure can disrupt sleep
  • Customisable Timers — set your target immersion and shiver duration from the watch or Garmin Connect app settings
