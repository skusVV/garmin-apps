Cold Plunge Pro: Ice Bath & Shiver Protocol

Watch App Cold Exposure Recovery

Cold Plunge Pro is a guided cold exposure app for compatible Garmin watches. It guides you through the immersion phase and the crucial natural re-warming (shiver) phase — then tracks your progress over time so you can see your cold tolerance building session by session.

Features

  • Guided Protocol — seamlessly transitions from preparation through cold immersion into a dedicated shiver phase
  • Live Heart Rate — tracks HR min / avg / max on your wrist throughout the plunge
  • Streak Tracking — daily streak with a 1-day grace period so one missed day won't reset your momentum
  • Weekly Goal — set a weekly cold-time target in minutes; the This Week screen turns green when you hit it
  • All-Time Stats — total sessions, total lifetime cold time, average session, and personal best
  • Milestone Celebrations — recognised at 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, and 500 sessions, and 1, 5, 10, 25 hours of total cold exposure
  • Garmin Connect Integration — every session saved as a Strength Training activity with custom immersion and shiver fields
  • Smart Alerts — warns if you're plunging late in the evening when cold exposure can affect sleep quality
  • Customisable Timers — set target immersion and shiver duration from the watch or Garmin Connect settings

Cold Plunge Pro is for wellness tracking purposes only. Cold water immersion carries risks including cold shock, hyperventilation, and cardiovascular stress. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting cold exposure practices, especially if you have any pre-existing medical conditions.

Frequently asked questions

What is the shiver protocol?

After cold water immersion, the body naturally generates heat through thermogenesis, which often includes shivering. Cold Plunge Pro guides you through a dedicated natural re-warming phase after your plunge rather than jumping straight into a hot shower, which is associated with additional metabolic and neurological benefits in cold exposure research.

Does Cold Plunge Pro save sessions to Garmin Connect?

Yes. Every session is saved as a Strength Training activity in Garmin Connect with custom fields for immersion time and shiver time, including the full FIT file with heart rate data.

What is the streak grace period?

Cold Plunge Pro gives you a 1-day grace period in your streak — one missed day will not reset your streak count, so you don't lose your progress over a single off day.

Does it warn about evening plunges?

Yes. Cold Plunge Pro shows a smart alert if you start a session late in the evening, as cold exposure before sleep can affect sleep quality for some people.
