Outdoor Readiness

Watch App Outdoor Weather

Before you head out the door, Outdoor Readiness answers a simple question: is it a good time to be outside? It checks five environmental factors — UV index, air quality, heat, wind, and rain — and tells you which one (if any) is the current limiter.

The forecast view shows the best activity window for the next 12 hours, so even if conditions are poor right now, you can see when they improve and plan your training accordingly.

Features

  • "Good to go" indicator based on all five factors combined
  • Identifies the current limiting factor (UV, air, heat, wind, or rain)
  • 12-hour forecast with best activity window highlighted
  • Location-based via coordinates set in Garmin Connect
  • Separate detail view for each environmental factor
  • Now view for current conditions at a glance
