Outdoor Readiness — Training Conditions Score

Watch App Outdoor Weather

Outdoor Readiness answers a simple question: is it safe and comfortable to exercise outside right now?

Open the app and instantly see a 0–100 readiness score — green when conditions are great, red when they're not. No guessing, no switching between weather apps.

What's behind the score

  • Heat — based on heat stress, not just air temperature
  • Air Quality — real-time AQI from PM2.5, ozone, and NO₂
  • Rain — intensity and probability combined
  • Wind — calm to dangerous, scored on a continuous scale
  • UV — sun exposure risk from low to extreme

Each factor is scored independently so you can see exactly why conditions are good or bad, and which factor is holding the score down.

Plan ahead

  • 24-hour forecast chart shows how conditions evolve
  • Best training window highlighted automatically
  • Glanceable from your watch face, detailed when you need it

How it works

  • Set your location once in Garmin Connect settings
  • App fetches weather and air quality data, scores it, and caches results
  • Fast, battery-friendly, and works on Forerunner, Fenix, Epix, Enduro, Instinct AMOLED, Venu, vívoactive, and MARQ series
  • Requires phone connection for data sync
