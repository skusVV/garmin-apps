Outdoor Readiness — Training Conditions Score

Watch App Outdoor Weather

Outdoor Readiness is a training conditions app for compatible Garmin watches. It answers a simple question: is it safe and comfortable to exercise outside right now? Open the app and instantly see a 0–100 readiness score — green when conditions are great, red when they're not. No guessing, no switching between weather apps.

What's behind the score

  • Heat — based on heat stress index, not just air temperature
  • Air Quality — real-time AQI from PM2.5, ozone, and NO₂
  • Rain — intensity and probability combined
  • Wind — calm to dangerous, scored on a continuous scale
  • UV — sun exposure risk from low to extreme
  • Each factor scored independently — see exactly which one is limiting your score

Plan ahead

  • 24-hour forecast chart shows how conditions evolve throughout the day
  • Best training window highlighted automatically
  • Glanceable from your watch face, detailed view when you need it

Outdoor Readiness scores are for informational and wellness planning purposes only. Environmental data is sourced from third-party providers and may not reflect exact real-time local conditions. Not a substitute for professional safety judgement or official weather warnings.

How to install Outdoor Readiness on your Garmin watch

  1. Open the Garmin Connect IQ Store listing for Outdoor Readiness
  2. Tap Try to start the 48-hour free trial
  3. Sync your Garmin watch via the Garmin Connect app
  4. Open the app on your watch — the score loads automatically
  5. Set your location in Garmin Connect → My Apps → Outdoor Readiness → Settings (latitude and longitude)

Frequently asked questions

How is the outdoor readiness score calculated?

The score combines five environmental factors: heat stress index, air quality index (PM2.5, ozone, NO₂), rain intensity and probability, wind speed, and UV index. Each is scored independently on a 0–100 scale, then combined into one overall score. The app shows which factor is currently limiting your score.

Does Outdoor Readiness work without internet?

Outdoor Readiness fetches weather and air quality data during a phone sync. Once synced, the cached score and forecast are available on your watch. Data refreshes automatically on the next sync — typically when you open the Garmin Connect app.

How do I set my location?

Set your coordinates (latitude and longitude) once in Garmin Connect → My Apps → Outdoor Readiness → Settings. The app uses these for all weather and air quality data requests.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Outdoor Readiness includes a full 48-hour free trial. After the trial, unlock forever for $3.99 — one-time payment, no subscription.
